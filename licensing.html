Licensing

Licensing Application.

For caregivers who already hold basic training and clinical hours, ready to become fully licensed with CGTIA.

Who Can Apply

Qualifications

  • 1 Completed CGTIA Certificate, or an equivalent recognized basic caregiver training.
  • 2 A valid lifesaving / first-aid certification.
  • 3 At least 72 hours of completed supervised clinical placement.
  • 4 At least 18 years of age, with a valid means of identification.
Before You Apply

Rules & Regulations

  1. All submitted documents must be authentic, current, and legible.
  2. False, altered, or expired documents will result in automatic disqualification.
  3. Your selfie photo must be a clear, recent likeness — no filters, sunglasses, or heavy editing.
  4. Applications are reviewed in the order received; incomplete applications will not be processed.
  5. The licensing fee is non-refundable once payment has been verified.
  6. Approved applicants will be contacted for a short verification interview before final licensing is issued.
